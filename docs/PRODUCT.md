# SmartMoney Product

## Product Description

SmartMoney is an affiliate cashback platform.

Users browse stores and offers, open affiliate links through SmartMoney, complete purchases on merchant websites, and earn cashback after the merchant validation or return period is completed.

## Main Goal

Deliver a working MVP to the client and create a maintainable cashback business.

## MVP User Flow

```text
Register
    ↓
Login
    ↓
Browse stores and offers
    ↓
Click affiliate link
    ↓
Purchase on merchant website
    ↓
Affiliate transaction received
    ↓
Cashback pending
    ↓
Return period completed
    ↓
Cashback confirmed
    ↓
Withdrawal requested
    ↓
Admin processes payment
